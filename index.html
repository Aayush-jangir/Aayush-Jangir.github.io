ABOUT

I'm Detrear Yaeger, an indie game developer with a deep passion for building compelling game experiences from the ground up. I hold a Bachelor of Technology and am currently completing an Advanced Diploma in Game Development specializing in both Unity and Unreal Engine.

My philosophy is simple: build first, polish second. I thrive in the prototyping phase — turning a design idea into a playable experience as fast as possible, then iterating relentlessly. Every project I ship teaches me something new about systems design, physics, and player psychology.

I'm equally at home in Unity's C# ecosystem and Unreal's Blueprint visual scripting, giving me the flexibility to choose the right tool for the right game.
